FIMS Admission Form

Admission Form Guidelines

To begin the school admission procedure, please fill out the form below and make sure that the information provided is complete and accurate. Please note that you can apply for admissions in preschool, junior school, and senior school.

You may select the city and the nearest FIMS campus from the dropdown menus. In case of any query, please comment in the relevant section.

On submitting the application, the concerned campus will contact you with further information.
